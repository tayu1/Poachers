HOW TO PLAY

  • Movement: Select a piece. Valid moves are highlighted in green, attacks in orange, King captures in red.
  • Control: You can only move your team's pieces that lie on your half of the board.
  • Cross line: Once a piece crosses the center line, your teammate controls it on their turn.
  • Combats: Attacking triggers poker evaluation. (Currently auto-resolved until Phase 2).
  • Pawn Flanks: Pawns on red flank squares cannot attack each other.
  • Kings: Cannot cross the half-line or touch enemy Kings.
